Honey Plaintains

    Ingredients

    Also known as plantain rogue, plantain temptation is a typical dish from the Caribbean regions. This delicious and traditional dish is very easy to make and although its taste is sweet, it is usually served as an accompaniment to meals.

    • 3 units of over ripe plantains(if they have a if the peal is black color even better)
    • 3 tablespoons plant base butter or margarine
    • 6 tablespoons of brown sugar or half a sweet panela
    • 6 cinnamon sticks

    Steps

    1. Cut each banana into 4 pieces. Saut√© in butter, turning constantly. Let them brown on all sides. The plantain for this recipe is also known as plantain macho.
    2. Add water to cover them halfway. Add brown sugar or panela and cinnamon sticks and bring to a boil.
    3. Wait until the mixture is thick, like a caramel. The bananas should look dark from the honey, and they should be fully cooked. Combine the temptation plantains with rice and meat.
