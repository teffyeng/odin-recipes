Mamallena Pie

Mamallena Pie

    Ingredients

    In Panamanian cuisine mamallena is a common dessert made from stale bread, suet, egg, sugar or golden syrup, spices, and dried fruit.
    My recipe is totaly vegan

    • Bread
    • Almond Milk
    • Sugar
    • Nutmeg
    • Ground flaxseed
    • Cinnamon
    • Raisins
    • Margarine or Plant base butter
    • vanilla

    Steps

    1. Place the bread in cubes in a large mixing bowl and some milk and set aside.
    2. Add all the remaining ingredients to a blender and blend until smooth.
    3. Pour the liquid mixture all over the bread, stir, and set aside for about 1 hour at room temperature.
    4. Preheat the oven to 350ºF or 180ºC.
    5. Place the pudding mixture in a baking dish and bake for 60 to 70 minutes.
    6. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly.
